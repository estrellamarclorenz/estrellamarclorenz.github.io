ABOUT US

Belcof is a cozy café booth pairing premium iced coffee with authentic, caramelized Belgian waffles. As a welcoming community hub, it offers a high-quality, delicious escape from the daily grind.


OUR HISTORY

Born from a tiny coffee booth and one waffle iron, Belcof celebrates the perfect harmony of premium espresso and caramelized Belgian waffles. We still serve that same comforting experience today.
